About me...

Originally from south of Chicago, I, along with my trusty feline sidekicks, now reside in Marietta, Ohio. In my spare time, which I have very little of while learning how to create websites, I enjoy practicing Pilates. I am working through the Power Pilates System training. I will be fully certified by the end of summer 2018.

In addition to my pilates practice and teacher training, I have set myself the daunting goal of running the Pittsburgh half marathon in 2019. But my favorite, and least strenuous pastime is enjoying a good mystery novel. I'm always open to reading recommendations!
