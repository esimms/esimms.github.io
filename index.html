Hello and Welcome...

Welcome to my website. I am a GIS professional who enjoys pilates, a good book, and a cup of tea in no particular order. I have always enjoyed maps and love that I get to spend my time creating and learning more about them.

I manage the GIS department for Eureka Midstream an oil and gas midstream company. Eureka Midstream operates in the Appalachian Basin. I enjoy the challenge that supporting the fast-paced and ever changing environment of a midstream oil and gas company brings.

I have been a spatial database nerd since my first introductino to GIS. I've wanted to get into the mobile and web development side of GIS as it is both super interesting and the future of GIS. I'm excited to dive head first into my second master's program. I am working my way into the world of cartography and web development through the GIS and Web Map Programming online program at UW. Slowly but surely I'm learning; enough for this website anyway.
